# Sales Mastery Skill

## Description
Master the art and science of closing deals with Alex Hormozi's proven sales frameworks. This skill provides actionable scripts, 16 closing techniques, and response time strategies that transform leads into customers.

## Core Principles

### 1. Price Signaling
> "If you're closing four out of five people, 80% or more, it means you're probably underpriced by three to four X."

When close rate exceeds 80%, you are leaving money on the table. High close rates signal demand exceeds your pricing power.

### 2. Speed Wins
> "HubSpot did a study - contacting leads 5 minutes vs 30 minutes after opt-in = 100x more likely to connect."

Response time is the single biggest lever in sales. AI and automation are now essential for beating competitors to the lead.

### 3. Customer Obsession
> "You will never go out of business focusing on the customer."

Every decision should filter through: "Does this help my customer?" When in doubt, serve.

### 4. AI as Sales Partner
> "AI is gonna start beating salespeople earlier than you'd think - they get to leads faster and follow up perfectly."

The salespeople who thrive will use AI as a force multiplier, not fear it as a replacement.

### 5. Process Over Talent
> "The reverse always works" - warm processes on cold leads fails, but cold processes on warm leads succeed.

A good cold process beats a great warm process every time.

### Framework 2: Lead Response Protocol

```
MINUTE 0-5:  [HIGHEST VALUE]
- Auto-respond with personalized message
- Trigger AI to gather context on lead source
- Queue follow-up sequence

MINUTE 5-30:
- Human touch for high-value leads
- Reference their specific comment/question
- Book demo immediately

MINUTE 30-60:
- Second wave automated sequence
- Social proof injection (case studies)
- Scarcity element if applicable

HOUR 2+:
- Break up sequences into value-first content
- Never lead with pitch
```

## Scripts & Templates

### Script 1: The Instant Response Template

```
Subject: Thanks for reaching out, [Name]!

Hey [First Name],

Saw your message come in and wanted to respond personally.

A few quick questions to make sure I point you in the right direction:

1. What's your biggest challenge with [their stated problem]?
2. When you imagine the perfect outcome, what does it look like?
3. What's stopping you from solving this today?

I'll have some specific recommendations ready for our call.

[Link to calendar - 15 min slot within 24 hours]

Talk soon,
[Your Name]
```

### Script 2: The "I Need to Think About It" Close

```
"I completely understand - this is a smart decision to think through.

Here's what I usually tell people in your situation: [pause]

The people who wait typically end up paying more because [price goes up / demand increases / problem gets worse].

The people who move forward usually tell me one thing got them over the hump - they realized the cost of NOT solving this was higher than the cost of solving it.

So let me ask you this: If money wasn't the issue, would you do this today?

[If yes] Then let's figure out how to make the money work.

[If no] What else is holding you back besides the money?"
```

### Script 3: The Closer or Further Close

```
"I want to check something with you before we wrap up.

Does this conversation get me closer to or further from where I'm trying to go?

[Let them respond]

I'm asking because [specific benefit] would mean a lot to me, and I want to make sure I'm not wasting either of our time if there's a fit here.

What would it take for us to move forward today?"
```

### Script 4: The Price Objection Response

```
"I hear you on the investment. Let me ask you something:

If this was $500 less, would you do it today?

[If yes] Okay, so price is the only thing. Here's what I can do...

[If no] Help me understand what's else is going on.

But here's what I know: [Competitor] charges 3x more and people still choose them because [specific outcome]. We're pricing this way because we want to make this accessible, but I can only do that if we're both serious.

What would it take for you to feel confident this is the right decision?"
```

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Don't use warm processes on cold leads** - You'll only capture the "six inch putts" - easy yeses. The rest will feel sold to.

2. **Don't let response time exceed 5 minutes** - Every minute past 5 reduces contact probability by 10%.

3. **Don't negotiate on price before understanding value** - Always anchor to transformation, not features.

4. **Don't pitch without diagnosing first** - "Tell me about your situation" before "Let me tell you about us."

5. **Don't fear AI replacing salespeople** - Use it to reach leads faster and follow up perfectly. The human touch comes after.

6. **Don't close at 80%+ without testing price increases** - You are leaving 3-4x on the table.
