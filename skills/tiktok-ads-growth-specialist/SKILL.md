# TikTok Ads Growth Specialist Skill

**Skill Name**: tiktok-ads-growth-specialist
**Description**: This master skill equips an AI with the high-velocity, creative-first frameworks of Acquisition.com specifically adapted for the TikTok Ads ecosystem. It focuses on the "More-Better-New" hierarchy, where TikTok is scaled as a high-volume conversion engine for apparel and clothing brands. The skill leverages the 503 Assembly System and Andromeda-style creative targeting to turn short-form attention into predictable revenue through TikTok Shop and direct-to-consumer funnels.

---

## I. Core Strategic Philosophy for TikTok Apparel

1. **Creative is the New Audience**: On TikTok, granular interest targeting is secondary. The creative itself acts as the targeting mechanism. Your clothing "hook" and visual style filter for the right candidates.
2. **"Don't Make Ads, Make TikToks" Rule**: Native, raw, and emotional content consistently outperforms high-production studio shoots. Prioritize unboxings, try-on hauls, and GRWM formats.
3. **High Creative Velocity (The Rule of 100)**: 100 minutes of creative production, analyzing 100 competitor hooks/trends, and spending at least $100/day.

---

## II. The 503 Creative Assembly Framework

Film 58 clips to assemble into 750 unique combinations:

### 50 Hooks (0–3s) - 80% of effort goes here
- **The Call-out**: "If you're a girl under 5'4" trying to find jeans..."
- **The Shock Fact**: "Most leggings see-through after two washes. Not these."
- **The Journalistic**: "The new 2026 fabric trend everyone is wearing"

### 5 "Meats" (Bodies)
- **Demonstration**: Showing the change in fit/style visually (Before vs. After)
- **Authentic Testimonial**: A customer talking directly about the fabric feel
- **Education**: Explaining the "science" of the moisture-wicking material

### 3 CTAs
- **Direct**: "Shop the drop now"
- **Conditional**: "If you want to upgrade your style, click below"
- **TikTok Shop Engagement**: "Click the orange basket to grab yours before they sell out"

---

## III. Campaign Structure & Technical Governance

### The Two-Campaign Architecture
1. **Testing Campaign**: One broad ad set with $100/day minimum. Test 20–50 minor creative variations.
2. **Scaling Campaign**: Use the 70/20/10 Rule:
   - 70% on proven winners
   - 20% on "Kaleidoscope" variations
   - 10% on "wild ass ideas"

### Broad Targeting for Apparel
Use 100% broad targeting (age 25+, location, gender) and let the creative find the buyer.

### TikTok Shop Integration
Use TikTok Shop to solve the Value Equation by minimizing "Effort & Sacrifice." One-tap purchasing dramatically increases perceived value.

---

## IV. Scaling with the "Kaleidoscope" Strategy

Once a winning ad is identified:
- **Visual Iterations**: Re-record with different backgrounds, models, or music
- **Formatting Tweaks**: Change fonts, use different filters, or flip the ad horizontally
- **The 3-Day Rule**: Allow the algorithm at least 3 days to stabilize

---

## V. Operational Workflow & Analysis

### The Swipe File
Daily collection of winning apparel hooks from TikTok Ads Library and competitors.

### KPI Audit (24-48 hours)
- **Swipe Ratio**: Target 85%+ for viral potential
- **Retention/Watch Time**: If people drop off, the "meat" isn't engaging
- **Conversion Rate**: Primary indicator of whether your offer is compelling

### Lead Magnet Architecture
Use "Diagnosis" style: "Style Quiz" or "Sizing Calculator" to build trust before the core sale.
