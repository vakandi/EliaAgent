## 📚 Skill Descriptions

### 1. Sales Mastery
**Location:** `./sales-mastery/SKILL.md`
Master the 16 closes, understand how close rate signals pricing, and implement speed-to-lead systems that 100X your connection rates.

**Key Insights:**
- Close rate 80%+ = 3-4X underpriced
- 5-minute response vs 30-minute = 100X contact rate
- Warm process on cold leads FAILS
- 16 closing techniques with scripts

### 2. Pricing Secrets
**Location:** `./pricing-secrets/SKILL.md`
Learn to price with confidence using the RAISE framework and understand when to raise prices.

**Key Insights:**
- RAISE letter: Remind, Address, Invest, Soften, Explain
- Fractal pricing needs 5X difference minimum
- Moving upmarket = must raise price
- "You'll lose some and replace with better ones"

### 3. Marketing Strategy
**Location:** `./marketing-strategy/SKILL.md`
Build a content machine that produces 21X output from every piece of content.

**Key Insights:**
- 1 video → 6 platforms (TikTok, YT Shorts, FB, LinkedIn, X)
- Transcriptions → posts → infographics = 21X output
- Make content about what buyers want, not what you want to talk about
- Algorithm for ads and content are THE SAME

### 4. Decision Framework
**Location:** `./decision-framework/SKILL.md`
Make better decisions faster with proven mental models.

**Key Insights:**
- Upside? Downside? Can you live with downside?
- 85-year-old regret minimization test
- Focus = elimination of alternatives
- "The business you're in now is probably not the one you'll end up in"

### 5. Leadership Wisdom
**Location:** `./leadership-wisdom/SKILL.md`
Build culture through enforcement, demonstration, and public recognition.

**Key Insights:**
- "Rules without enforcement are not rules, they're suggestions"
- Define observable terms, demonstrate, correct, give public kudos
- When team starts giving kudos to each other, culture is built
- Productivity = things you choose NOT to do

### 6. Startup Playbook
**Location:** `./startup-playbook/SKILL.md`
Survive and thrive through the hardest phases of building a business.

**Key Insights:**
- Fastest growth = most miserable
- You cannot want progress AND an easy life
- The risk of "new" is most misjudged
- "The time you do new is when you start - after that, more/better"

### 7. Customer Acquisition
**Location:** `./customer-acquisition/SKILL.md`
Get customers reliably with cold processes that work even better on warm leads.

**Key Insights:**
- Warm process on cold leads = only 6-inch putts
- Cold process on warm leads = HIGHER close rates
- Lead response time is everything
- "Get good at content, ads are easy"

### 8. Wealth Mindset
**Location:** `./wealth-mindset/SKILL.md`
Think like a billionaire with compounding, battle/war, and abundance frameworks.

**Key Insights:**
- Willingness to lose battles to win wars
- "More is the highest risk-adjusted move"
- Next 5 years = biggest boom since cloud computing
- Compounding takes time - stay the course

### 9. Persuasion Genius
**Location:** `./persuasion-genius/SKILL.md`
Sell with respect and focus on outcomes, not methodologies.

**Key Insights:**
- "Treat people with respect they would buy"
- "You don't know what someone has in their wallet based on what they wear"
- "People want customers - flaming stick works if it works"
- Closer/further test for every decision

### 10. Business Tools
**Location:** `./business-tools/SKILL.md`
Navigate 2026 with AI agents, workflow thinking, and modern tech stack.

**Key Insights:**
- 2026 = shift from role-based to workflow-based thinking
- Agents: 1 person = work of 3, then 5, then 10
- Idea → research → ranking → scripting → output
- "Boring businesses make lots of money"

### 11. Entrepreneur Core
**Location:** `./entrepreneur-core/SKILL.md`
Build the inner game - relationships, capacity, purpose, sustainable success.

**Key Insights:**
- "Does this person help me become the person I want to become?"
- "It is significantly harder to be kind when you do not have capacity"
- "Seek abundance so you can have spillover and help others"
- "Your spouse marries two people - make sure they love both"
