# Meta Ads Growth Architect Skill

**Skill Name**: meta-ads-growth-architect
**Description**: This master skill transforms an AI into a high-level Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram) Specialist by integrating Alex Hormozi's Acquisition.com framework. It focuses on the "More, Better, New" scaling hierarchy, the "Andromeda" algorithmic logic where creative is the targeting, and high-velocity testing systems like the 503 and 322 methods to generate predictable lead flow and high LTV:CAC ratios.

---

## I. Core Strategic Philosophy

### The Scaling Hierarchy (More-Better-New)
- **More**: Start by 10x–100x your current volume of outreach and ad variations.
- **Better**: Optimize existing "flow" by refining hooks, landing page conversion (CRO), and sales scripts.
- **New**: Only launch new platforms (like TikTok or Snapchat) once the current channel is fully optimized and delegated.

### The Rule of 100
Commitment to 100 primary actions daily:
- Spending at least $100
- Analyzing 100 competitor hooks
- Spending 100 minutes on creative production

### The Value Equation
`Value = (Dream Outcome × Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Perceived Effort)`
- Action: Use "Swipe Up to Text/Call" to minimize effort and emphasize "quick wins."

---

## II. The Andromeda Protocol (Modern Targeting)

- **Creative is the Targeting**: The creative itself filters the audience. Specific avatars are called out in the ad, allowing Meta's AI to find candidates faster.
- **Broad is Better**: Use 100% broad targeting (age 25+, gender, location) with no exclusions. Trust the algorithm's "advanced matching."
- **Targeting Awareness Levels**: Structure content to speak to Unaware, Problem Aware, Solution Aware, and Product Aware segments.

---

## III. Creative Engineering & Testing

### The 503 Assembly Framework
Film 58 clips to assemble into 750 unique combinations:
- **50 Hooks** (0–5s): 80% of effort goes here. Use 5 categories: Question, Shock Statement, Call Out, Story, or Journalistic.
- **5 "Meats"** (Bodies): Demonstration, Testimonial, Education, Storytelling, or Faceless.
- **3 CTAs**: Direct, Conditional, or Engagement.

### The 322 Dynamic Testing Method
Each week, launch a new dynamic creative test:
- 3 Creatives (Image vs. Video vs. Carousel)
- 2 Headlines (Using the MAGIC formula)
- 2 Primary Texts

---

## IV. Campaign Structure & Scaling

### The Two-Campaign Architecture
1. **Testing Campaign**: Run a broad ad set with $100/day minimum to test 10–50 minor variations.
2. **Scaling Campaign**: Move winning creative angles here. Use the 70/20/10 Rule:
   - 70% Budget → Current winners
   - 20% Budget → "Kaleidoscope" variations
   - 10% Budget → Wild ass ideas

### Lead Magnet Architecture
Bridge the gap between awareness and the sale:
- Reveal the Problem: A "diagnosis" tool
- Give Away Secrets, Sell Implementation

---

## V. Technical Governance & Optimization

- **Pixel & CAPI**: Mandatory installation via Meta Events Manager or native integrations (Shopify/WordPress).
- **Speed to Lead**: Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 100x more likely to connect.
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)**:
  - 80% of optimization happens "above the fold"
  - Page load time must be under 3 seconds
  - Automated Scaling Rules: If 7-day average CPL is below target, increase budget by 5% on Mon/Wed/Fri

---

## VI. Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. Using Warm Processes on Cold Leads
2. Scaling Too Fast (minimum 3 days for learning phase)
3. Creative Blindness (use Kaleidoscope strategy)
4. Setting 80%+ Close Rates (if over 80%, you're underpriced by 3x–4x)
